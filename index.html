Programmer of our Capstone Project Our capstone project is titled "Research Innovation Monitoring and Evaluation System." The goal of the project was to develop a web application that streamlines the process of conducting research online. The application includes a wide range of features and tools to support the research process. These include tools for managing research proposals, tracking the progress of research projects, organizing research materials, and facilitating collaboration among research team members.

The project involved collaboration across different courses, including web development, research methods, and project management. Throughout the project, we applied our skills and knowledge to develop a solution that addresses a real-world problem.

To ensure the effectiveness of the application, we conducted extensive testing and evaluation with users. The feedback we received from these tests allowed us to refine the design and functionality of the application to better meet the needs of users.

Overall, the project was a success, and the application has the potential to significantly improve the process of conducting research online.
